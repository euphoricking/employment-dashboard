Employment Dashboard: Austria and Neighboring Countries (2014–2023)

This dashboard allows you to explore employment trends in Austria and its neighboring countries (Germany, Italy, Switzerland, Hungary, Czechia, Slovakia, Slovenia). Select a year and comparison country to view employment indicators side by side.

  • Left Panel: Select a year for Austria and view Male, Female, and Total employment rates with trends.
  • Right Panel: Select a comparison country and year. Indicators update accordingly.
  • Map: Hover over any country to see employment rates for the selected year.
  • Charts: Line charts show trends; bar charts compare Austria and the selected country.
